About

We are a family business located in Libiąż. We have been on the market since 2004. For 13 years, we have provided people with a range of baby accessories. Our store was created for people. We put a lot of stress on the individual approach to each client.

For those purchasing our furniture or baby strollers, we also provide basic training on how to assemble and use it. If you are looking for something that is not in stock right now we probably can order it for you.

Offer

Clothes

We offer clothes for children from all age range (from 0 to around 12 years old). We offer various collections depending on the season.

Accesories

We also offer a wide range of baby accessories.In our shop you can buy teats, baby bottles, rattles, chews, carousels, pacifiers, bibs.

Strollers

In our offer you can also find baby carriages, car seats, baby cots as well as whole sets of children's furniture.
